Biography

Dr. Yongkuk Jeong is an Assistant Professor at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, specializing in sustainable production logistics. His research combines AI, digital twins, and simulation to transform the field of production and logistics. He is passionate about leveraging cutting-edge technologies to create innovative solutions that drive the next generation of manufacturing.

Research Interests

Dr. Jeong's research focuses on the digitalization of production logistics, leveraging technologies such as AI and digital twins to optimize manufacturing systems. He is particularly interested in:
